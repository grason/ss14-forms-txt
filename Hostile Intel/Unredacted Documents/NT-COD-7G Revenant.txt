To Station Command Authority,

This document is classified under Clause 7-G of the [bold]Corporate Secrets Mandate[/bold], effective immediately upon retrieval. It supersedes standard protocol regarding disclosure, interdepartmental transparency, and ethical containment.

Confirmation has been received of a [color=#4cc1c1]Revenant[/color]-class incorporeal anomaly presently active aboard your station. Clearance is hereby extended to acting command and security personnel under emergency authority.

[bold]INTELLIGENCE SUMMARY:[/bold]
[bullet]Non-corporeal being of unknown origin, invisible to unaided observation.
[bullet]Feeds off electrical systems, psychological distress, and organic life.
[bullet]Possesses powers including EMP bursts, mental disruption, and entropy induction.
[bullet]Not bound by material laws—passes through walls, security, and vacuum.

[bold]THREAT ASSESSMENT:[/bold]
[bullet]Invisible to crew without specialized scanning tools.
[bullet]Destabilizes equipment, causes power surges and psychic breakdowns.
[bullet]Incapacitated crew are often drained of vital essence.
[bullet]Appears to grow in strength with each victim.

[bold]RESPONSE DIRECTIVES:[/bold]
Issue station-wide alert under pretense of electrical grid fault.
Deploy science and security collaboration to detect presence.
Use spiritual, experimental, or arcane technology to anchor and purge.
Limit crew panic; do not disclose full capabilities unless absolutely necessary.
