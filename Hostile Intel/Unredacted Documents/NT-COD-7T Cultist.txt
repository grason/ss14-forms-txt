To Station Command,

This document is classified under Clause 7-T of the [bold]Corporate Secrets Mandate[/bold], effective immediately upon retrieval. It supersedes standard protocol regarding disclosure, interdepartmental transparency, and ethical containment.

Confirmation has been received of a [color=#7d1e3d]Cultist[/color]-class theological infiltration cell presently active aboard your station. Clearance is hereby extended to acting command and security personnel under emergency authority.

[bold]BEHAVIORAL INDICATORS:[/bold]
[bullet]Whispers in Common or unknown script; blood rituals in chapels or hidden rooms.
[bullet]Apparent reanimation, teleportation, and “runed” artifacts.
[bullet]Construction of desecrated altars or "Soul Stones."
[bullet]Cultists refer to each other using code phrases and ranks (e.g. “Acolyte,” “Herald”).

[bold]STATION RISK ASSESSMENT:[/bold]
[bullet]Resurrection of personnel through anomalous means.
[bullet]Psychological instability or mass hallucination events.
[bullet]Existential breach potential if Nar’sie is summoned.
[bullet]AI interference and cloning blockages likely.

[bold]CONTAINMENT AND RESPONSE:[/bold]
[bullet]Isolate and terminate suspected cultists immediately.
[bullet]Destroy all altars, tomes, and soul anchors.
[bullet]Deny access to chaplain’s quarters or library if corrupted.
[bullet]Contain the chaplain—no personnel are exempt from screening.

[color=#7d1e3d][bold]IF THE FINAL RITUAL BEGINS, STATION SELF-DESTRUCT PROTOCOL IS AUTHORIZED UNDER CLAUSE T-5.[/bold][/color]
