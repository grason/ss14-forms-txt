[bullet]Entity class presents with necrotic appearance and aggressive behavior.
[bullet]Transmission occurs through bodily fluids, typically via bite.

[bold]THREAT ASSESSMENT:[/bold]
[bullet]Spread is exponential if uncontained.
[bullet]No cure or inoculation currently approved by NanoTrasen Biomed Division.
[bullet]Personnel tend to underestimate risk due to initial sluggishness.
[bullet]Structural integrity risk: doors, vents, and hatches can be compromised.

Instruct medical personnel to cease revival attempts on infected.
Authorize decapitation and full cremation of any suspected vectors.
