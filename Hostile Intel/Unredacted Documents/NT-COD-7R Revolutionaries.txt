To Station Command Authority,

This document is classified under Clause 7-C of the [bold]Corporate Secrets Mandate[/bold], effective immediately upon retrieval. It supersedes standard protocol regarding disclosure, interdepartmental transparency, and ethical containment.

Confirmation has been received of a [color=#b32d29]Revolutionary[/color]-class insurrectional movement presently active aboard your station. Clearance is hereby extended to acting command and security personnel under emergency authority.

[bold]INTELLIGENCE SUMMARY:[/bold]
[bullet]Revolutionary-class threats consist of NanoTrasen personnel radicalized or subverted into insurrectionist activity.
[bullet]Command-level officers are the primary targets of subversion efforts.
[bullet]Entities utilize improvised arms, syndicate-sourced encryption, and psychological coercion.
[bullet]Operations are decentralized and rapidly coordinated via mesonic and bluespace uplinks.

[bold]OBJECTIVES:[/bold]
[bullet]Removal or execution of command personnel.
[bullet]Transfer of station control to unauthorized revolutionary authority.
[bullet]Destabilization of essential systems, morale, and NanoTrasen policy enforcement.

[bold]OPERATIONAL INDICATORS:[/bold]
[bullet]Coordinated attacks on command and bridge sectors.
[bullet]Disruption of communication lines, AI subversion, or unauthorized detainment of leadership.
[bullet]Distribution of coded phrases, makeshift armbands, or falsified ID credentials.
[bullet]Unusual alliances forming among otherwise non-cohesive crew sectors.

[bold]CONTAINMENT AND NEUTRALIZATION PROTOCOLS:[/bold]
[bullet]Command personnel must not travel alone. Assign armed escort when feasible.
[bullet]Authorization for [bold]lethal suppression[/bold] of identified revolutionaries is granted without tribunal.
[bullet]Brig integrity and communication systems must be prioritized and held at all cost.
[bullet]Purge and audit all PDA message logs for signs of coordination.
[bullet]If resistance becomes systemic, Command is authorized to request emergency AI override or station lockdown under Directive Theta-Six.

[color=#1C3E91][bold]FAILURE TO CONTAIN A REVOLUTIONARY-CLASS EVENT WILL RESULT IN ASSET TERMINATION, LIABILITY INVERSION, AND TOTAL DATA PURGE UNDER CLAUSE 10-F.[/bold][/color]
