To All Command and Security Assets,

This document is classified under Clause 7-C of the [bold]Corporate Secrets Mandate[/bold], effective immediately upon retrieval. It supersedes standard protocol regarding disclosure, interdepartmental transparency, and ethical containment.

Confirmation has been received of a [color=#4eb36b]Blob[/color]-class self-replicating biomass presently active aboard your station. Clearance is hereby extended to acting command and security personnel under emergency authority.

[bold]ENTITY PROFILE:[/bold]
[bullet]Spontaneous emergence via bluespace rupture or failed containment crate.
[bullet]Highly adaptive; exhibits resistance to conventional ballistic and thermal weapons.
[bullet]Substructures generate lesser entities (e.g. spores, nodes, cores) which support macro-expansion.
[bullet]Not sapient—no negotiation or compromise is possible.

[bold]ECOLOGICAL DAMAGE:[/bold]
[bullet]Assimilates materials (plasteel, carbon, biomass) for growth.
[bullet]Disrupts APCs, powernets, and air integrity.
[bullet]Rapid growth rate—complete deck absorption in under ten minutes recorded.

[bold]CONTAINMENT DIRECTIVE:[/bold]
[bullet]Prioritize location of central core via crew tracker or visual cue (bright green).
[bullet]Deploy flamethrowers, emitters, and anti-blob munitions.
[bullet]Station nanite systems should be disabled to avoid self-assimilation.
[bullet]Evacuate civilians from growth zone and initiate hull breaches to slow spread.

[color=#4eb36b][bold]FAILURE TO CONTAIN A BLOB-CLASS ENTITY IS A LEVEL-ONE BIOLOGICAL FAILURE.
EXTERMINATION OF ALL ORGANIC LIFE MAY RESULT.[/bold][/color]
